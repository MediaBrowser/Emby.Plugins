Google Drive Accounts

Connect one or more Google Drive accounts.

Instructions

1. Open Google Drive
2. Select a Project or create a new one
3. Click "Go to Credentials"
4. Click "New Credentials" -> "OAuth Client Id"
5. If you're asked to configure the consent screen, click "Configure Consent Screen".
6. On the consent screen, select your email address, enter product name "Emby", then submit.
7. On the Create Client Id screen, click Application Type Other, then click Create.
8. You'll be returned to the main screen, where your Client Id and Client Secret will be displayed. Copy these values.
9. In the left sidebar, click "Overview". Then under APIs & Services, click on "Dashboard".
10. We now enable the "Google Drive API"
11. Enter the Client Id and Client Secret values here and click Save.
12. Add one or more google drive accounts using the form by clicking the "+ Add" button..
13. Fill in a DisplayName and click the "Grant Access" button.
14. Sign in with the same account as you did the previous steps.
15. Allow the application to View & Manage Google Drive Files
16. You receive a code. Copy this code into the "code" field in the Form and press "Save."
You now have your Google Drive available in Emby!
